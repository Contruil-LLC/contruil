## Short Description (50–75 words)

Contruil gives enterprises visibility, control, and a verifiable audit trail across their entire AI estate without replacing existing infrastructure. We function as a supervisory control plane across AI providers, integrating at the API layer — classifying requests by business purpose and sensitivity, applying human-gated validation for sensitive actions, and producing independently verifiable control logs suitable for internal review, customer attestation, and external audit.

---

## Long Description (150 words)

Contruil LLC provides supervisory AI control infrastructure for API-driven financial platforms operating multi-model AI environments under audit and vendor risk pressure.

**What we do:** Policy-based AI classification at the API boundary. Supervisory monitoring across multi-model workflows. Human-gated validation for sensitive production actions. Independently verifiable control logs for customer and auditor review. Executive-ready governance summaries for internal and board-level stakeholders.

**Who it's for:** Platform Engineering, Developer Experience, Security & Infrastructure, Risk & Compliance, and Vendor Risk & Attestation Owners at financial services institutions and infrastructure fintech platforms.

**Deployment:** Change-managed 30–60 day pilots. No model replacement. No vendor lock-in. Designed for high-change AI environments that still require audit defensibility.

**IP:** U.S. Provisional Patent Application No. 63/980,310 (filed February 11, 2026) — patent pending.
